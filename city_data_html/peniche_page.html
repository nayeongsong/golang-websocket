Peniche is like my digital nomad dream spot. It's got chill vibes with waves as my work soundtrack. Living here won't break the bank, and the surf scene is lit. Exploring cool historic spots and vibing with friendly locals make my remote work life feel like a chill adventure.

Peniche, a classical destination for old school nomads, is a serene and idyllic hideaway that has seemingly been overlooked by the digital nomad community. The tranquil and peaceful environment contrasts dramatically with the hustle and bustle of popular cities, making it a refreshing alternative for nomads seeking peace and quiet to focus on work.

Co-working space, Largo, is the digital nomad's hub in Peniche. This co-working space offers a comfortable working environment with stable, high-speed internet, and a cozy ambience that is perfect for productive hours of work. Coupled with a small yet friendly community of like-minded nomads, it offers the perfect balance between work and social life.

But work isn't all there is to Peniche. This town offers a plethora of fun and healthy activities that can complement the nomadic lifestyle. Famous for its surfing conditions, Peniche is a surfer's paradise, with numerous surf schools for beginners and challenging waves for experienced surfers. If you're more into exploring underwater worlds, there are exciting diving spots in Berlenga that showcase mesmerizing marine life and underwater caves.
