Lake Balaton has a range of neighbourhoods, each brimming with its unique attractions. Swimming, fishing, and sailing are the staples here, with the lake's calm waters inviting all and sundry. Despite its charm, Lake Balaton hasn't become a hotspot for digital nomads. A peculiar charm of some parts of Lake Balaton is that they feel as if they've been preserved in amber since 1989. This has both its pros and cons.
