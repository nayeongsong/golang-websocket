Sargodha (Punjabi and Urdu: سرگودھا) is a District and capital of Sargodha Division, located in Punjab province, Pakistan. It is Pakistan's 12th largest city by population[14] and one of the fastest-growing cities of the country. Largest producer of Citrus and also known as California of Pakistan. Very good city with great hospitality. Great people. Sargodha is also known as the City of Eagles.
