hangten


I had a good time here. The internet at my stay was terrible for gaming. Most restaurants had awful food, but a few were decent: Hangten, Nomad Café, and Rise. Hangten is a great place for breakfast; beautiful rooftop view.

There aren’t many cars, so renting a bike for daily commutes was awesome. Outpost is a solid spot to work, though it gets really busy during "hot seasons." I went in Sep and it was pretty quiet.

I stayed for two weeks and wouldn't recommend staying longer. There aren't many tourist spots, though there are some nice neighboring cities for weekend trips.
