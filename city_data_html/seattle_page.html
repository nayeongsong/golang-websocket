Seattle is a great city, especially if you like nature, there are plenty of outdoor activities to do all year round. People are generally open-minded and friendly. The cost of living is high but it allows you to be able to afford other places on earth easily if you can find a job here. You can also find plenty of good restaurants and arts around in the city. I recommend to stay at least 3-6 months to get the feel of living a PNW life.

Seattle is an awesome city, very acceptive and progressive! The weather is not bad most months of the year, when the sun is out it's one of the most beautiful places in the country. People are very friendly but it often takes time for them to warm up. Besides that the economy is booming and there's plenty of jobs available! It's a large city with other great cities in the metro that are suitable for families such as Bellevue, Redmond, Issaquah, Kirkland and so on. Safe overall for a large city and super safe cities close by. Plenty of amenities and a great food scene as well. It's an expensive metro to live in but very worth it if you can get a job here.
