Great place to stay overall.
Pros:
- Cheap
- Safe
- Lots of variety. Not too big, not to small of a city
- Great weather between December and February
- Very efficient ride-sharing apps like Bolt.
- Reasonable-priced round-trip plane tickets from Europe (especially if you opt for a stopover)
Cons :
- Meh weather except December-February
- Beware of motorcycles and tuk-tuk drivers, they drive recklessly.
- Difficult to get things shipped from Amazon and western retail websites in general
- Can be noisy in a lot of places. Carefully pick your accommodation.
