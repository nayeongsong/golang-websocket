Cagliari is not that Nomad friendly but once you adjust, you like the staying. Being concrete:
1) In cafés you are expected to order, drink the coffee and get out, not to chill for half day
2) People will not speak English even if they knew, they still answer in Italian
3) You can't go swimming or gyming without a doctor's certificate that you're eligible for that activity.... you may buy cigarretes without doctor's appointment though :-/
4) Renting houses / cars is expensive, especially in summer
5) You are not allowed to sleep at the beaches nor to camp anywhere else except tha camping paid places
6) Even McDonalds wifi won't work without linking with your social network account
