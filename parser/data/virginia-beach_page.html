2 years ago
Safe and clean. Typical peak tourist season in the summer. Some fun events during the year like the Neptune festival, sandcastle and jet ski competition. This is not your funky beach community vibe, but more of a planned commercial district. Restaurants are average and the surf fishing can be great. Norfolk International Airport is close but usually a bit more expensive flights than say BWI. Just north of VA Beach you can cross the modern marvel bay bridge tunnel and travel back in time 50 years to Cape Charles which is a quaint beach town on the Eastern Shore of Virginia. South of VA Beach you could also find Sandbridge which is like what Corolla or Duck, North Carolina use to like before the tourists discovered them.
