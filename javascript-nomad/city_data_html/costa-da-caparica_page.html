6 months ago
I spent a month in Caparica and I really loved my time there. The vibe is amazing, the community was growing by that time and it was already very good and welcoming, I felt welcomed and part of something :) Dr. Bernard was one of my favorite spots and their surf classes were suuuuuper great, amazing surf teachers with a really nice vibe. Also, the food is amazing all around Caparica with a lot of options. Really looking forward to go back and spend more time in Caparica with the amazing community :)

Dr Bernard is the place to work, Koa café the best food and there's daily events.
