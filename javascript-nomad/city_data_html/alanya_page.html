An interesting place which has a decent Russian-speaking community from post-Soviet republics.
Pros:
- quite affordable
- proximity to the sea/beach (plenty of condos 2-4 mins walking distance)
- Fiber Internet (50mbps is easy to get)
Cons:
- limited food options (Turkish cuisine)
- really slow at times (might be a pro for some)
- no coworking spaces
