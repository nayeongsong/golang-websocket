I lived in Chongqing for about 5 years. I mostly love this place but it's not easy for Westerners unless you can speak some Chinese. But with a smartphone you can translate anything you need to say. Chongqing is beautiful, gritty and romantic. Extremely inexpensive and lots of things to do. Beautiful women. Maybe the most unique city in China. Weather terrible in winter and summer. Winter is foggy, rainy and low light while summers are uncomfortably hot and humid. They don't speak standard Mandarin here so even Chinese from Beijing can't talk to the locals. I had the time of my life here but it's too strange and foreign for many. Not like going to Thailand or the Philippines. It took a while to grow on me.
